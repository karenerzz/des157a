user testing results

  • overlays

    The overlays didn’t reset when the slide was changed and that is one of the main points my testers brought up. However, I saw that a lot of my testers closed the overlay before moving onto the next slide.

  • information button

    My testers hesitated to click on the information button or didn’t click on the button at all. So the information button definitely went unnoticed when I wanted it to be interacted with.

  • slideshow dot indicator

    All my testers were interacting with the slideshow dot indicators so I thought that was interesting. Through questioning, I realized that changing a slide through its indicator is a very popular interaction.

improvements

Based on these insights and other feedback, I plan on resetting the overlays when the slides change and making the information button more in frame with the other content. Additionally, I plan on fixing design inconsistency issues such as the buttons.
